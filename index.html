About Us

Powering Professional Kitchens Across Telangana

Telangana ProKitchen is a Hyderabad-based manufacturer and supplier of commercial kitchen equipment. We design, fabricate and supply high-quality equipment to hotels, restaurants, cloud kitchens, hospitals and catering businesses across the state.

With over a decade of experience, every product we deliver is built to withstand the demands of professional kitchen operations — backed by on-site installation, staff training and after-sales support.
